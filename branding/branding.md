# Branding Colors

- Primary: Purple (#800080ff)
- Secondary: Goldenrod (#d4aa00ff)
- Background: White (#f9f9f9ff)

Text should generally be black on the white background.

The Primary color should be used for things like backgrounds of headers and buttons when the buttons are displayed on the White Background as defined. The Primary color should also be used to identify actionable items such as links when text is displayed on the white background.

The Secondary color should be used for icons, hyperlinks, and other actionables. Text when placed on the Primary color should be displayed in the Secondary color.

The background should be the White as defined above.
